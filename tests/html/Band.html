About Us

Stage Fright is a 2021 American rock band formed in Columbus, Ohio. Our band consists of Lead Vocalist Aidien Gooden, Lead Guitarist Sean Kalb, Drummer Jabari Hill, and Lead Bassist Gabe Zeigler. Stage Fright was founded by Aidien and Sean later meeting Jabari and Gabe and inviting them to join the band. The four of them made a few songs together, performing at as many events and places as they could. This caught the attention of TR Records. Our debut album, "Acantha," was released on September 16, 2021, by TR Records.
