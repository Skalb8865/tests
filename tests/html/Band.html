About Us

Stage Fright is a 2021 American rock band formed in Columbus, Ohio. Our band consists of Lead Vocalist Aidien Gooden, Lead Guitarist Sean Kalb, Drummer Jabari Hill, and Lead Bassist Gabe Zeigler. Our debut album, "Acantha," was released on September 16, 2021 by TR Records.

Band Member History

Aidien Gooden

As a child Aidien has had a talent for singing, So realzing this his father signed him up for his local churches choir. He would contiune this path until he became the lead vocalist of Stage Fright.

Sean Kalb

In middle school Sean played the guitar at his local school jazz band and stuck with it. As Sean got older he wanted to expand his career and with the help of Aidien they formed the rock band Stage Fright.

Jabari Hill

Jabari loved playing the drums as he was growing up he played in drumlines and joined his school jazz band. One day wanting to put his skills to use he was invited by Sean and Aidien to join Stage Fright.

Gabe Zeigler

Gabe went to many rock concerts as a kid and he quickly fell in love bass guitar. Playing in local resturants near his home town wanting for more Gabe joined Stage Fright in order to show his bass skills to the world.
